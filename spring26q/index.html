UIUC Quantum Systems Seminar is a weekly meetup for anyone interested in or wants to learn more about quantum systems research at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

Our aim is to build a community of quantum systems researchers and enthusiasts at UIUC, and provide a platform to share knowledge, ideas, and collaborate on quantum systems research.

We discuss recent significant work (e.g., published at top-tier conferences) and identify new research directions/ideas.

Join us on Slack: uiuc-sysnet.slack.com and search for channel #quantum-systems-seminar
