Spring 2026

UIUC Quantum System Seminar is a weekly meetup for faculty and students who are interested in learning about quantum system research at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

The goal is to build a community of quantum system research and provide a platform to share knowledge, ideas, and collaborate on research projects.

We discuss recent significant work and identify new research directions and ideas.

Join us on Slack: uiuc-sysnet.slack.com and search for channel #quantum-systems-seminar
